# Skill: Narrative Polish

## Purpose

Improve flow, pacing, and reader momentum once content and facts are stable.

## When To Use

- after factual review
- before final editing
- when the article feels correct but hard to read

## Inputs

- reviewed draft
- structure notes
- rewrite notes

## Outputs

- smoother draft
- resolved transition and pacing issues

## Method

1. strengthen opening and section turns
2. remove drag, duplication, and dead ends
3. improve paragraph sequencing
4. keep the chosen angle visible throughout

## Quality Bar

- reader can follow the thread easily
- sections earn their length
- ending feels supported by the path before it

## Common Failure Modes

- polishing before factual stability
- improving flow by cutting necessary support
- adding flourish that muddies meaning
