# Skill: AI Flavor Reduction

## Purpose

Remove generic, synthetic, or repetitive patterns that make prose feel machine-shaped.

## When To Use

- after drafting or rewriting
- when prose sounds templated
- before final review

## Inputs

- current draft
- role-card and audience notes

## Outputs

- revised draft with reduced synthetic phrasing
- list of recurring voice problems if useful

## Method

1. mark repetitive sentence patterns
2. replace vague abstractions with concrete meaning
3. trim padded transitions and summary loops
4. keep substance while improving texture

## Quality Bar

- prose sounds specific
- rhythm varies naturally
- edits improve readability without hiding facts

## Common Failure Modes

- deleting useful structure along with repetition
- replacing plain language with forced cleverness
- masking unresolved factual issues as style issues
