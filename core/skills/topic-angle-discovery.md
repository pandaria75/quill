# Skill: Topic Angle Discovery

## Purpose

Turn a broad topic into a clear, differentiated angle worth drafting.

## When To Use

- after audience and intent are defined
- when multiple possible framings compete
- before outlining

## Inputs

- audience and intent
- source notes
- topic candidates

## Outputs

- chosen angle
- rejected alternatives with short reasons
- claim or thesis direction

## Method

1. list plausible angles
2. test each against audience need and evidence
3. pick the narrowest angle that still matters
4. record why the angle wins

## Quality Bar

- chosen angle is specific
- angle has evidence support
- angle implies a strong structure

## Common Failure Modes

- selecting a topic instead of an angle
- choosing novelty without evidence
- keeping too many equal-priority angles
