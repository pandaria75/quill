# Skill: Style Adaptation

## Purpose

Match tone, density, and explanation style to the intended audience and outlet.

## When To Use

- after a workable draft exists
- when publication context matters
- before final editing

## Inputs

- draft
- role-card
- audience and publication expectations

## Outputs

- revised draft with aligned tone
- style notes for remaining edits

## Method

1. compare draft voice to audience needs
2. adjust jargon, pacing, and examples
3. preserve meaning while changing delivery
4. note any style tradeoffs made

## Quality Bar

- style fits the intended reader
- technical accuracy survives simplification
- tone stays consistent

## Common Failure Modes

- flattening useful nuance
- overfitting to a trendy voice
- changing claims while changing tone
