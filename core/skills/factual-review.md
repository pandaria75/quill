# Skill: Factual Review

## Purpose

Check whether claims are supported, scoped correctly, and honest about uncertainty.

## When To Use

- after a draft exists
- before finalization
- whenever evidence quality is in doubt

## Inputs

- draft
- context pack
- cited or referenced sources

## Outputs

- review notes
- claim corrections or uncertainty flags

## Method

1. inspect major claims
2. verify support and scope
3. mark unsupported, overstated, or stale assertions
4. record what must change before finalization

## Quality Bar

- important claims are traceable
- uncertainty is visible
- no invented authority or false precision

## Common Failure Modes

- checking wording instead of substance
- allowing implication to outrun evidence
- ignoring edge cases that alter meaning
