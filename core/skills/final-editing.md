# Skill: Final Editing

## Purpose

Prepare the article for delivery after content, structure, and factual quality are already acceptable.

## When To Use

- at the final gate
- after review issues are resolved
- before publication handoff

## Inputs

- reviewed draft
- rewrite notes
- final gate checklist

## Outputs

- final article
- short record of last changes if needed

## Method

1. fix grammar, clarity, and formatting
2. ensure headings, links, and emphasis are consistent
3. confirm all placeholders are resolved or intentionally kept
4. produce the final handoff file

## Quality Bar

- article is publication-ready
- formatting is consistent
- no unresolved placeholders remain by accident

## Common Failure Modes

- treating copy edits as a substitute for review
- introducing meaning changes late
- missing leftover TODOs or unsupported claims
